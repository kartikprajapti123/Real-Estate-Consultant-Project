Privacy Policy

Introduction

We take the protection of your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal data when you visit our website and interact with our services.

1. Data Collection

We collect personal data when you visit our website, contact us, or use our services. This includes information such as:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • IP address
  • Browser type and version

2. Purpose of Data Collection

We use your data for the following purposes:

  • To respond to your inquiries and provide customer service
  • To improve our website and services
  • To communicate important information regarding your inquiries

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties without your consent, unless one of the following cases occurs:

  • If required by law or legal process
  • If we need to protect our legal interests
  • For fraud prevention or investigation purposes

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

5. Cookies

Our website uses cookies to enhance your experience. Cookies are small files stored on your device. You can control cookie settings through your browser.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. You may withdraw your consent to data processing at any time. To exercise these rights, please contact us using the email address provided below.

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary for the purposes described in this policy or as required by law.

8. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time. If we make changes, we will update the date at the top of this page.
