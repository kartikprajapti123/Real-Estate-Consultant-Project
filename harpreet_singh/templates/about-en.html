About Me

My name is Harpreet Singh Ghotra, and real estate is not just my profession—it’s my passion.

Discipline and responsibility have shaped my life from the start. My grandfather served honorably in the Indian Army, instilling in me the values of integrity and dedication. Inspired by these principles, I joined the German Army (Bundeswehr) at 18. That experience taught me structure, resilience, and accountability—qualities I now apply to my work every day.

In 2020, during the COVID-19 pandemic, my father introduced me to real estate. Around that time, a transformative book deeply shifted my perspective. Energized by this new vision, I acquired my first three multi-family properties within just a few months.

Over the past five years, I’ve gained hands-on experience navigating the rewarding and challenging aspects of real estate. Today, my mission is to support others—especially members of the Indian community in Germany—as they pursue their own property goals.

I understand the difficulties people face—language barriers, unfamiliar processes, and complex systems—because I’ve been there. My aim is to make the journey smooth and transparent, offering honest guidance and step-by-step support.

I’m dedicated not only to helping you buy property, but to empowering you to achieve clarity, confidence, and long-term financial security through smart planning and a trusted network.

Let’s build something meaningful together—for you, your family, and your future.
