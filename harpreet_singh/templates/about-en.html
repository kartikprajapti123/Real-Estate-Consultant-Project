About Me

My name is Harpreet Singh Ghotra, and real estate is not just my profession—it is my passion.

Discipline and responsibility have shaped my life from the very beginning. My grandfather served honorably in the Indian Army, embodying values of integrity and dedication that inspire me to this day. Motivated by these principles, I joined the German Army (Bundeswehr) at 18. That experience instilled in me structure, resilience, and the confidence to take responsibility—qualities I bring to my work every day.

In 2020, during the height of the COVID-19 pandemic, my father introduced me to the world of real estate. Around the same time, a transformative book profoundly changed my perspective. Fueled by this new vision, I purchased my first three multi-family properties within just a few months.

Over the past five years, I have gained hands-on experience navigating both the rewarding and challenging aspects of real estate. My mission now is to support others—especially members of the Indian community here in Germany—as they pursue their own real estate goals.

Many individuals face obstacles with language barriers, complex processes, and unfamiliar systems. I understand these challenges firsthand. My goal is to make the journey straightforward and transparent, providing honest guidance and step-by-step support.

I am committed not only to helping you acquire property but also to empowering you to build clarity, confidence, and long-term financial security through strategic planning and a trusted network.

Let’s create something meaningful together—for you, your family, and your future.
